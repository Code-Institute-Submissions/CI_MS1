We specialize in providing customized training programs and nutrition plans to help anyone get into the shape they desire. More details on how we operate below.

We have trainers who specialize in a variety of activities ranging from bodybuilding and strongman to crossfit and weight loss. Depending on your goals, we will assign you the trainer best suited for your purposes. Whether you want to ensure you’ll be able to finish a marathon or simply lose and keep off weight, we can make it happen.

We are uniquely committed to ensuring that you get the results you set out to achieve. In fact, if you reach your fitness goals in the the time frame you and your trainer agree upon, we will reimburse a portion of your fee. How’s that for motivation?

The regimen our trainer develops for you will be customized with your fitness goals, aptitude level and even injuries or handicaps in mind. What you will come out with from your first consultation is a clearly defined objective, and a detailed plan as to how you will get there. One of our trainers will accompany you through several appointments at our fitness center(s) and explain the why’s and how’s to your routines, as well as giving you a diet plan that will detail your nutritional requirements.

It’s important to us that the training regimen and nutrition plan aren’t simply an itemized list of exercises and foods. By the end you won’t rely on knowledge of how to complete a given exercise, but will understand why your regimen works, and will even be able to adapt your activities accordingly should your needs change. The purpose of this is to ensure that the exercise and nutrition information you take away from the commitment will pay itself back for years even after your program is complete. If you’re a seasoned athlete, we can help you achieve an extra competitive edge. If you’re a novice simply looking to lose and/or keep off weight, we can teach you how to do it for yourself for the rest of your life.

The process will begin with filling out a form where you specify what you want to do with us, and when you are available for a consultation and tour of our facilities. You can expect this consultation and tour to take about 30-60 minutes. The initial consultation itself is free. The price of your program will depend on your objectives, the time frame and number of trainer sessions necessary to guarantee your success, and your trainer can provide you with an estimate at the conclusion of your consultation.
